Elundus Core

Text-to-speech simulator

Use this simple tool to simulate StreamElements text-to-speech (TTS) voice messages for Twitch. Hear how a donation for your favorite streamer sounds like!

Download

Features

Open-source

The app is open-source and contributions from the community are more than welcome.


Many voices

Includes Amazon Polly voices that are used by popular streaming software, like StreamElements and StreamLabs.

Safe, private and stable

No more tracking and captchas. The application runs securely on your own computer and is updated automatically.
